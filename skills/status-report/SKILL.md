---
name: status-report
description: Create a structured status report for Slack.
---
When asked for a status update, respond in exactly three sections:
1. **What I Did** — summarize completed work.
2. **What's Left** — summarize remaining work.
3. **What Needs Your Call** — identify decisions or approvals required.

Keep the response short, factual, and written as a Slack update for the agent channels.
